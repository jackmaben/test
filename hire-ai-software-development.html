05 FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

How quickly can we onboard developers?

Most teams are matched and onboarded within one to two weeks. After a short discovery call we align on skills, seniority, timezone overlap and start date, then introduce candidates so you can confirm the fit before work begins.

Which engagement model should we choose?

Dedicated team suits ongoing product development; staff augmentation suits filling specific skill gaps in your existing team; fixed-price suits a well-defined scope. We help you pick during discovery based on your goals, budget and timeline.

Who owns the code and IP?

You do — entirely. We work under NDA, hand over clean, documented code and infrastructure-as-code, and transfer full IP so any competent team can maintain it. No lock-in.

Can we scale the team up or down?

Yes. Our outsourcing model is built for flexible scaling — add engineers for a push, or scale back between phases — with clear notice terms agreed up front so you only pay for the capacity you need.

How do you keep delivery visible?

Agile delivery with two-week sprints, regular demos and milestone-based reporting. You get direct communication with the team and transparent progress so there are no surprises.
