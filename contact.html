02 FAQ

Before you reach out.

Is the discovery call really free?

Yes — the initial 60-minute discovery call is free and carries no obligation. Its purpose is to understand your needs and see whether we're a good fit for each other.

We're not sure exactly what we need yet — is that ok?

Completely. Many clients come to us with a problem rather than a spec. Helping you clarify the right approach is exactly what discovery is for.

Do you work with clients outside India and the USA?

Yes — we have teams in Gujarat, India and California, USA, and work with businesses worldwide, remotely and on-site as needed across time zones.

How quickly can a project start?

For well-defined needs, we can often begin a scoped engagement within a couple of weeks of the discovery call and proposal sign-off.
